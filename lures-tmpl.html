Below are some of my favorite lures and methods

If you don't know how to set up a Texas Rig, please watch this video.

Straight Tail Roboworm is one of my favorite worms. These worms are very versatile and can easily be texas rigged but are also great for a dropshot rig

Crankbaits are awesome when the water temp is down a bit and you need to get down deeper where the fish are. Work is slow along the bottom and you are bound to catch fish.

Spinnerbaits are very good for catching bass. There are so many color options so do your best to match the bait fish in the lake you are fishing

Kinky Beaver, don't let the name fool you. These baits work amazing when you are working a lot of cover (brush piles, lay downs). Always use a Texas Rig with a Kinky Beaver.

Just remember, these are only a few of my favorite lures and methods. There are countless ways to catch bass and countless lures out there to try. Be sure to try a few of these methods out but don't be scared to try some of your own ideas out!
